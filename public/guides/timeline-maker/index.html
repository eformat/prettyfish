Most useful Mermaid Timeline patterns

  • timeline declaration: Declare a timeline diagram
  • title: Set the timeline diagram title
  • section: Group timeline events under a labeled section
  • single event: Single event at a time period
  • multiple events: Multiple events at same period separated by colons
  • multi-line event: Split event across multiple lines with continuation
